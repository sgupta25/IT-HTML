Baby Driver (2017)

One of my favorite movies is Baby Driver. It's about a getaway driver teenager who's always listening to music. He needs to because he has tinnitus
from the car accident his parents died in when he was young. He's the best at what he does, so he's
on every job and isn't allowed to stop driving even after he's paid off his debt to a mob boss. It's
not a long movie, but it's so good from start to finish. It's directed by Edgar Wright, so the
cinematography really stood out and made the movie even more enjoyable for me.
