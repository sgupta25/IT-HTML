The show has completed 6 seasons.
Season 7 will air starting May 2020.

The worldwide law-enforcement organization known as S.H.I.E.L.D.
(Strategic Homeland Intervention Enforcement and Logistics Division)
employs an elite team of agents who investigate strange occurrences
around the globe and beyond. Its members -- each of whom brings a
specialty to the group -- work to protect those who cannot protect
themselves from extraordinary and inconceivable threats, including
a formidable group known as Hydra.
